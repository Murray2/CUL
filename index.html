Alberta Apprenticeship has resumed First Period Apprenticeship Exams

The first semester begins with introductory and intermediate level courses, while the second semester is designed as an advanced level and builds upon previous skill development. Harry Ainlay's Culinary Arts Program:

Total Weighting Semester I

I. Peer Evaluation & Instructor Evaluation 80%

II. Sanitation Exam & Evaluation 10%

III. Final Menu Item Practical Exam 10%

Total Weighting Semester II

I. Peer Evaluation & Instructor Evaluation 90%

II. Career Directions & Expansions 10%

III. Opportunities and Apprenticeship Preparation 0%

*Focus will be on major curricular outcomes due to COVID-19.

Culinary Arts: Year 1


Every student has to complete the Alberta Government Sanitation Examination.

  • CKA3900: Apprenticeship Safety
  • CKA3401: Kitchen Orientation
  • CKA3406: Kitchen Mathematics
  • CKA3421: Cooking Proteins 1
  • CKA3441: Fruits & Vegetables
  • CKA3446: Starches & Salads
  • CKA3451: Breakfast & Dairy
  • CKA3456: Introduction to Baking
  • CKA3461: Breads & Cookies
  • CKA3466: Pies & Fillings

Culinary Arts: Year 2


Semester two continues to hone and develop the skills created in semester one.

  • CKA3436: Thickeners & Sauces
  • CKA3431: Stocks & Soups
  • CKA3411: Meat & Seafood 1
  • CKA3416: Meat & Seafood 2
  • CKA3426: Cooking Proteins 2
  • CTR2310: Career Directions-Expansion
  • CKA3560: Practicum A
  • CKA3565: Practicum B
  • CKA3570: Practicum C
  • CKA3575: Practicum D
